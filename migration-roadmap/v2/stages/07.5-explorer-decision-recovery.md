# Stage 7.5: Explorer Decision Recovery

Goal: add Explorer-specific recovery semantics after the generic Stage 7 decision
and escalation contract exists.

## Planned Behavior

- Map `needs_user_decision` to backend-owned pending decisions.
- Support `none_of_above` and user refinement as a controlled rediscovery path.
- Support `escalate_discovery` by raising `EXPLORATION_GAP`; the generic
  escalation policy decides whether that means rediscovery.
- Define escalation events that fail closed when policy cannot produce a valid
  rewind or artifact state is invalid.
- Keep canonical Explorer publication and Knowledge Lifecycle integration
  deferred to Stage 9 unless explicitly promoted earlier.

## Exit Criteria

- Recovery paths are represented in backend contracts, not frontend-only logic.
- Rediscovery preserves decision history and invalidates stale downstream
  artifacts before re-running phases.
- Tests cover user-decision, rediscovery through policy, and invalid escalation
  resolutions.
